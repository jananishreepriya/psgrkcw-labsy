Head Help & Guidance

📊 Dashboard

  • See your assigned labs, pending approvals, and approved bookings count.
  • Recent pending bookings are listed for quick action.

⏳ Pending Approvals

  • Review booking requests for labs under your purview.
  • Click Approve to send to admin for final approval.
  • Click Reject with a reason – staff will be notified.

📜 All Bookings (My Labs)

  • Filter by lab, status, and search by staff name or purpose.
  • Export filtered data to PDF or Excel.
  • Track all past and pending bookings.

🎫 My Support Requests

  • View all your submitted support queries with status (Pending/Resolved).
  • See when each request was resolved.
  • Click "Report Issue" to submit a new query to admin.

📧 Email Notifications

  • You receive an email whenever a staff member books a lab you supervise.
  • If admin approves a booking that conflicts with a class, the class head (you) will be notified.
  • Support request resolution also sends email.

💡 Tips

  • Always provide a reason when rejecting – it helps staff understand.
  • Use the search bar in All Bookings to quickly find specific requests.
  • Click "Report Issue" to send a query to admin – you'll get email when resolved.
