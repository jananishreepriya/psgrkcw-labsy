Admin Help & Guidance

📊 Dashboard

  • View total labs, staff, heads, today's bookings, and pending approvals.
  • Recent bookings and today's academic status are shown.

📅 Timetable Management

  • Add/Edit/Delete class schedules for each lab and day order (1-6).
  • Bulk upload timetable via Excel/CSV.
  • Timetable conflicts automatically block instant bookings.

🚫 Block Day

  • Mark specific dates as holiday, exam, etc. – no bookings allowed.
  • Affected class heads are notified automatically.

👥 Manage Staff & Heads

  • Add/Edit/Delete staff and academic heads.
  • Bulk upload via Excel/CSV (name, email, password).
  • Assign labs to heads.

🔬 Manage Labs

  • Add/Edit/Delete labs with name, description, capacity.

📆 Academic Calendar

  • Generate monthly calendar with automatic day order rotation.
  • Manually change day type (normal/holiday/exam) and day order.

📋 All Bookings & History

  • View all bookings, filter by status.
  • Approve/reject bookings (after head approval).
  • Export filtered history to PDF/Excel.

🛟 Support Queries

  • View all support requests from login page, staff, and heads.
  • Filter by status, source, date range, or search by name/email/message.
  • Click "Complete" to mark a request as resolved – the user receives an email notification.

📧 Email Notifications

  • Staff bookings notify heads + admin.
  • Head approval notifies admin.
  • Admin approval notifies staff.
  • Timetable clash notifications sent to respective HODs.
  • Support request resolution emails are sent to the requester.
