Staff Help & Guidance

🏠 Dashboard

  • View today's date, academic day order, and any important notices.
  • Quick actions: View Schedule, Book a Lab, My Bookings, Public Schedule.
  • Recent bookings are shown for quick reference.

📅 Lab Schedule

  • Select a lab and day order to see scheduled classes and available slots.
  • Green buttons (Book Period X) indicate free slots – click to instantly book.
  • If a slot has a scheduled class, you can still request approval from admin (who may cancel class).

📝 Book a Lab (Regular)

  • Choose lab, date, session (FN/AN/Full Day), and purpose.
  • System checks availability and shows any conflicts.
  • If no conflict, you can submit for head approval.
  • If conflict exists, you can request admin approval (which may override classes or instant bookings).

⚡ Instant Booking (Period-wise)

  • From Lab Schedule, click any green period button.
  • Enter purpose – if no conflict, booking is instantly approved.
  • If conflict exists, request is sent to admin for approval.
  • You'll receive email confirmation on approval.

📜 My Bookings

  • View all your bookings with status (pending/approved/rejected).
  • Filter by status and export to PDF/Excel.
  • Check admin remarks if rejected.

🎫 My Support Requests

  • View all your submitted support queries with status (Pending/Resolved).
  • See when each request was resolved.
  • Click "Report Issue" to submit a new query to admin.

🌍 Public Schedule

  • View lab availability – useful for planning.

📧 Email Notifications

  • You'll receive email when your booking is approved or rejected.
  • Instant bookings send confirmation immediately.
  • Support request resolution also sends email.

💡 Tips

  • If your request is pending, you can contact your lab head for faster approval.
  • Click "Report Issue" to send a query to admin – you'll get email when resolved.
